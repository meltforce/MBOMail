Features

Everything you'd expect from a mail app

All the mailbox.org features you love, with the native macOS integration you've been missing.

⚡

Instant access

Set a global keyboard shortcut to toggle MBOMail from anywhere, or click the menu bar icon. Your inbox is always one gesture away.

🔔

Native notifications

Get macOS notifications when new mail arrives — with sender and subject — even when the app is minimized or hidden.

🔒

Tracker blocking

Built-in protection against email tracking pixels and third-party cookies. Browse your inbox privately, without add-ons.

📄

Unread badge

See your unread count at a glance on the Dock icon, so you always know when new messages are waiting.

🎨

Custom appearance

Inject your own CSS to customize the look and feel of mailbox.org. Make it yours with custom themes and tweaks.

✉

Default mail app

Set MBOMail as your default email client. Clicking mailto: links anywhere on your Mac opens a compose window right in MBOMail.

🌑

Dark mode

Full dark mode support that follows your macOS system appearance. Easy on your eyes, day and night.

🔗

Link inspection

Hover over any link to see where it actually goes. Shortened URLs are automatically expanded so you can verify before clicking.

🔄

Auto-updates

MBOMail keeps itself up to date in the background. New features and fixes are delivered automatically.

Always within reach

Configure a global hotkey to bring up your inbox instantly, no matter what app you're in. The menu bar icon shows your unread count and lets you toggle the window with a single click. You can also set MBOMail to hide automatically when you switch to another app — it stays out of your way until you need it.

⌥ + M

Your inbox, your rules

Inject custom CSS to restyle the interface, block distracting elements, or build an entirely new look. MBOMail also supports custom JavaScript for power users who want to extend the functionality even further. Settings are organized in clear tabs for General, UI, Shortcuts, and Advanced options.

.folder-tree {
  background: #1a1a2e;
}
.mail-detail {
  font-size: 15px;
}

Know at a glance

The Dock icon badge shows your unread count without you having to open the app. Native macOS notifications display the sender and subject of new messages as they arrive, so you never miss anything important — even when MBOMail is in the background.
